## How to Replace Images

1. **Prepare your images**: Ensure images are optimized for web (recommended: WebP or PNG format, properly sized)
2. **Replace files**: Copy your images to the corresponding paths listed above
3. **Maintain naming**: Keep the same filenames or update the paths in `Home.vue` if you use different names
4. **Recommended sizes**:
   - Service icons: 80x80px or similar square format
   - Team photos: 300x300px or similar square format
   - Blog images: 400x250px or similar landscape format
   - About thumbnail: 500x600px or similar portrait format
   - Testimonial photos: 100x100px or similar square format
